About Me

My name is Tomasz Pluskiewicz

I was born in 1986 in Zabrze, Poland

In 2010 I graduated Computer Science (Data Processing System Engineering speciality) on the Electronics Faculty at Wrocław University of Technology. I gained a master degree, my thesis titled "Design the modelling methods of object database system using Sybase PowerDesigner tools"

I currently work as a Software Developer at Wrocław-based company PGS Software S.A.
